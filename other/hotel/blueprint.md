# Hotel Template

## Template Style: Hotel / Hospitality
- **Framework**: Bootstrap 5.0.0
- **Typography**: Heebo, Montserrat (Google Fonts)
- **Theme**: Primary Orange (#FEA116), Dark (#0F172B), Light (#F1F8FF)

---

## Sections
- **Navbar**: Sticky dark navbar with "Premium Version" button
- **Hero Carousel**: Slides with "Discover A Brand Luxurious Hotel" caption and buttons
- **Booking Form**: Horizontal form with Check-in/out, Adult/Child select
- **About Us**: Text content + 4-image grid + CounterUp statistics (Rooms, Staffs, Clients)
- **Our Rooms**: Grid of room cards with pricing, star rating, and amenities (Bed, Bath, Wifi)
- **Video**: "Luxury Living" text with a play button opening a YouTube modal
- **Our Services**: Grid of services (Spa, Gym, Event, etc.) with hover effects
- **Testimonial**: Owl Carousel with client reviews
- **Our Team**: Staff profiles with social media overlay
- **Newsletter**: Subscribe form
- **Footer**: Company info, Contact details, Services links, Copyright
