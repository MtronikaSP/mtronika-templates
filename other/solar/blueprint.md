# Solar Template

## Template Style: Solar & Renewable Energy
- **Framework**: Bootstrap 5.0.0
- **Typography**: Open Sans, Roboto (Google Fonts)
- **Theme**: Green (#32C36C), Dark (#1A2A36), Light (#F6F7F8)

---

## Sections

- **Navbar**: Sticky top navigation with "Solar" brand and links
- **Header Carousel**: Full-screen hero slider with solar energy imagery and captions
- **Feature**: Highlights (Pioneers of solar, etc.)
- **About Us**: Information about the company
- **Services**: Grid of services (Solar Panels, Wind Turbines, etc.) with icons
- **Projects**: Portfolio grid with Isotope filtering (Solar, Wind, Hydropower) and Lightbox
- **Quote**: Request a Free Quote form with image background
- **Team**: Team members with social links
- **Testimonial**: Client testimonials in a carousel
- **Footer**: Address, Quick Links, Project Gallery, Newsletter signup

## Key Features
- **Isotope**: specific filtering for Portfolio
- **Lightbox2**: For portfolio image viewing
- **Wow.js**: Scroll animations
- **CounterUp**: Number counters
- **Owl Carousel**: Used for Header and Testimonials
