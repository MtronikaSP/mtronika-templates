# Healthcare-Medical template_b_v1

## Template Style: DentCare - Dental Clinic
- **Framework**: Bootstrap 5
- **Typography**: Jost + Open Sans (Google Fonts)
- **Theme**: Dental blue primary with modern rounded styling

---

## Sections

- **Topbar**: Opening hours + Email + Phone + Primary accent shape
- **Navbar**: Tooth logo + Navigation + Search modal + Appointment button
- **Full Screen Search Modal**: Overlay search input
- **Hero Carousel**: Fullscreen image slider with CTAs (Appointment + Contact)
- **Banner Cards**: 3-column (Opening Hours, Search Doctor, Make Appointment)
- **About Section**: Text content + Image + Checkmarks (Award Winning, Professional Staff, 24/7, Fair Prices)
- **Appointment Form Section**: Primary background + Award text + Booking form
- **Services**: Before/After comparison slider + 4 service cards (Cosmetic, Implants, Bridges, Whitening)
- **Special Offer Banner**: 30% discount promotion section
- **Pricing Plans**: Owl Carousel with pricing cards
- **Testimonials**: Primary background carousel
- **Team/Dentists**: 6 team member cards with social links
- **Newsletter**: Signup form section
- **Footer**: 4-column layout (Quick Links, Popular Links, Contact, Social)
