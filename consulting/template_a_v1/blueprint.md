# Consulting template_a_v1

## Template Style: Insure - Insurance Company
- **Framework**: Bootstrap 5
- **Typography**: Roboto + Poppins (Google Fonts)
- **Theme**: Professional blue primary with light background

---

## Sections

- **Topbar**: Dark bar with Phone + Email + Hours + Social icons
- **Navbar**: Light nav with rounded bg + Icon logo + Navigation + "Get A Quote" button
- **Hero Carousel**: Image slider with headline + CTAs
- **About Section**: Years experience badge + Image + Flexible Plans + Money Back guarantee
- **Facts/Stats**: Split section - Left text + Right counter stats (Clients, Projects, Awards, Team Members)
- **Features**: 4 icon cards (Easy Process, Fast Delivery, Policy Controlling, Money Saving) + Image
- **Services**: 6 service cards (Life Insurance, Health Insurance, Home Insurance, Vehicle Insurance, Business Insurance, Property Insurance)
- **Appointment Section**: Award badge + Description + Booking form
- **Team**: 4 team member cards with hover social overlay
- **Testimonials**: Pulsing animated testimonial images + Owl Carousel
