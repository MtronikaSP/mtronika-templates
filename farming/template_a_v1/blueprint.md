# Farming template_a_v1

## Template Style: FarmFresh - Organic Farm
- **Framework**: Bootstrap 5
- **Typography**: Open Sans + Roboto (Google Fonts)
- **Theme**: Green primary + Orange secondary (organic/natural colors)

---

## Sections

- **Topbar**: Phone number + Centered Logo ("FarmFresh") + Social icons (Twitter, Facebook, LinkedIn, Instagram)
- **Navbar**: Green sticky nav + Mobile logo + Navigation links (Home, About, Service, Product, Pages dropdown, Contact)
- **Hero Carousel**: ID `#header-carousel` (Fullscreen image slides + Headlines + Dual CTAs: Explore + Contact)
- **Banner Cards**: 2-column (Organic Vegetables + Organic Fruits with background patterns)
- **About Section**: Left bordered image + Right content (100% Organic + Award Winning icons)
- **Facts/Stats**: Primary bg with 4 counter stats (Experience, Farm Specialists, Complete Projects, Happy Clients)
- **Services**: 5 service cards (Fresh Vegetables, Fresh Fruits, Healthy Cattle, Modern Truck, Farming Plans) + intro text + Contact CTA
- **Features**: Primary bg (100% Organic, Award Winning, Modern Farming, 24/7 Support) + Center white card with image
- **Products**: Owl Carousel product cards with images, names, prices, Cart/View buttons
- **Testimonials**: Background image section + Owl Carousel white text reviews
- **Team**: 3 farmer cards with side social icons column
- **Blog**: 3-column hover-overlay blog cards with titles + dates
- **Footer**: Primary bg with 3-column layout (Get In Touch, Quick Links, Popular Links) + Newsletter sidebar
- **Copyright**: Dark footer bar
