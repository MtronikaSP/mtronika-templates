# Template Blueprint: Retail E-Commerce Template A V1 (Fruitables)

## Template Style
- **Framework**: Bootstrap 5 (inferred from `bootstrap.min.css` and `data-bs-` attributes)
- **Typography**: Open Sans, Raleway (Google Fonts)
- **Color Scheme**:
    - Primary: Green (implied by vegetable theme, often `#81c408` or similiar in Fruitables)
    - Secondary: Orange/Yellow (Fruits)
    - Backgrounds: White, Light Gray, Dark (Footer)

## Sections
1.  **Header**:
    -   Top Bar: Contact info, Privacy/Terms links.
    -   Navbar: Logo (Fruitables), Navigation (Home, Shop, Pages, Contact), Search Modal trigger, Cart/User icons.
2.  **Hero Section**:
    -   Main Carousel: Hero images (Vegetables/Fruits).
    -   Search Box overlay.
3.  **Features**:
    -   4 Column Icons: Free Shipping, Security Payment, 30 Day Return, 24/7 Support.
4.  **Fruits Shop**:
    -   Tabbed Interface: All Products, Vegetables, Fruits, Bread, Meat.
    -   Product Grid with "Add to Cart" buttons.
5.  **Service/Featurs**:
    -   3 Promotional Banners (Fresh Apples, Tasty Fruits, Exotic Vegitable).
6.  **Vegetable Shop**:
    -   Owl Carousel displaying vegetable products.
7.  **Banner Section**:
    -   "Fresh Exotic Fruits" promo with detailed pricing and "Buy" button.
8.  **Bestseller Products**:
    -   Grid of bestseller items with star ratings.
9.  **Fact Counter**:
    -   Statistics (Customers, Quality, Certificates, Available Products).
10. **Testimonial**:
    -   Owl Carousel of client reviews.
11. **Footer**:
    -   Company info, "Why People Like us", Shop Info, Account, Contact.
    -   Copyright and Credits.
