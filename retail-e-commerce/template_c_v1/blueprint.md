# Template Blueprint: Retail E-Commerce Template C V1 (Believe)

## Template Style
- **Framework**: Bootstrap (implied by `bootstrap.min.css` and structure)
- **Typography**: Raleway, Roboto Slab, Playfair Display (Google Fonts)
- **Color Scheme**:
    - Primary: Blue/Dark Blue (`btn-blue`)
    - Secondary: Magenta (`btn-magenta`)
    - Backgrounds: White, Light Gray.

## Sections
1.  **Header**:
    -   Top Bar: Phone, Email (`info@bootstrapmart.com`), Offers ("Free Shipping"), Login/Reg links.
    -   Navbar: "Believe" Logo, Navigation (Home, Blog, Contact), Search Icon, Bag/Fav Icons.
    -   Mega Menu: Dropdown for MEN with sub-categories and featured products carousel.
2.  **Hero Slider**:
    -   Owl Carousel with promotional slides (Men's Fashion, Women's Fashion).
3.  **Category Banners**:
    -   3 Column Grid: Man Clothing, Woman Clothing, Accessories.
4.  **Newest Products**:
    -   Tabbed Interface: Featured, New Arrivals, Best Seller.
    -   Owl Carousel Grids for products within tabs.
    -   Product Cards: Image, Hover Icons (Heart, Retweet, Search), Price, Add to Cart.
    -   Fading/Hover effects on products.
5.  **Content Offers**:
    -   Full-width promo banner ("Tommy Hilfiger Women's").
6.  **Brands**:
    -   Carousel of brand logos.
7.  **Footer**:
    -   (Implied standard retail footer with links, newsletter, etc.)

## Additional Pages
-   `blog.html`
-   `blog-detail.html`
-   `contact.html`

## Key Features
-   **Owl Carousel**: Heavily used for Hero, Product Sliders, Mega Menu Products, and Brands.
-   **Mega Menu**: Complex dropdown with embedded product sliders.
-   **PrettyPhoto**: Lightbox for images.
-   **FontAwesome**: Iconography.
-   **Tabs**: Bootstrap tabs for product filtering.
