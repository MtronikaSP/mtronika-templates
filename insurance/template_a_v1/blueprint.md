# Insurance Template

## Template Style: Corporate / Finance
- **Framework**: Bootstrap 3.3.6
- **Typography**: Open Sans (Google Fonts), FontAwesome 4.6.3, Themify Icons
- **Theme**: Primary Cyan (#00b2ce), Dark Blue (#304254), Light Backgrounds

---

## Sections
- **Top Bar**: Address and social media links
- **Navbar**: Sticky header with "Call Us Now" CTA
- **Hero Slider**: FlexSlider with text overlays
- **Intro & Quote Form**: Welcome text alongside a floating "Get a free quote" form
- **Products**: Vertical tabs switching between Insurance types (House, Travel, Life, Car)
- **Services**: Grid of service features with icons
- **Testimonials**: Tabbed reviews with client photos
- **Recent News**: Blog post grid
- **Partners**: Owl Carousel of partner logos
- **Contact & Map**: Google Map embed + Contact details
- **Footer**: Multicolumn footer with Agent Details, Links, and Copyright

---

## Additional Pages
- `index.html` (Main Page options)
- `blog.html` (Blog listing)
- `blog-details.html` (Single blog post)
- `contact-us.html` (Contact page)

---

## Key Features
- **FlexSlider**: Main hero slideshow
- **Easy Responsive Tabs**: For Product switching
- **Owl Carousel**: For Partner logos
- **Sticky Navigation**: Fixed top menu
