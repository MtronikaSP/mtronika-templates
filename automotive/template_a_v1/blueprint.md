# Automotive template_a_v1

## Template Style: CarServ - Car Repair Service
- **Framework**: Bootstrap 5
- **Typography**: Barlow + Ubuntu (Google Fonts)
- **Theme**: Red primary (#D81324) + Dark secondary + Professional auto theme

---

## Sections

- **Spinner/Loader**: Animated loading spinner
- **Topbar**: Address + Hours on left | Phone + Social icons on right
- **Navbar**: Sticky white nav + Icon logo ("CarServ") + Navigation + "Get A Quote" CTA button
- **Hero Carousel**: ID `#header-carousel` (2 slides with headline, subtext, "Learn More" CTA + car images)
- **Quick Features**: 3-column icon cards (Quality Servicing, Expert Workers, Modern Equipment)
- **About Section**: Left overlapping image with years badge + Right content with numbered features + "Read More" CTA
- **Facts/Stats**: Dark bg with 4 counter-up stats (Years Experience, Expert Technicians, Satisfied Clients, Complete Projects)
- **Services Section**: Left vertical pill tabs (Diagnostic Test, Engine Servicing, Tires Replacement, Oil Changing) + Right tabbed content with image/description
- **Booking Form**: Secondary bg split layout (Left testimonial text + Right primary bg booking form: Name, Email, Service select, Date picker, Special Request)
- **Team/Technicians**: 4-column team cards with social overlay
- **Testimonials**: Owl Carousel testimonial cards with circular images
- **Footer**: Dark footer with 4 columns (Address, Opening Hours, Services links, Newsletter) + Copyright with footer menu
